# ZenithPlan AI 🌍✈️

ZenithPlan AI is an intelligent, eco-conscious travel planner and sustainable tourism companion. Designed for environmentally minded travelers, ZenithPlan AI empowers you to discover green destinations, map low-impact routes, and make travel choices that respect our planet.

---

## ☘️ Core Vision

Standard travel planners focus purely on cost and time. **ZenithPlan AI** redesigns the travel experience by introducing carbon-budget auditing, eco-certified discovery, and low-impact itinerary creation directly into your planning pipeline.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Eco-Spot Explorer:** Discover green spaces, sustainable accommodations, eco-certified parks, and local eco-initiatives across major travel destinations.
- **Carbon-Conscious Itineraries:** Create custom travel plans that favor low-carbon transit methods like trains, public transit, and shared micro-mobility.
- **Carbon Impact Estimator:** Estimate and visualize your travel carbon budget during planning, helping you offset or minimize your overall footprint.
- **Personal Green Dashboard:** Save sustainable routes, track your historical travel emission targets, and manage green itineraries from a unified personal profile.
- **Eco-Friendly Recommendations:** Receive tailored suggestions for regional zero-waste eateries, local green activities, and sustainable travel practices.

---

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy

ZenithPlan AI is designed with strict modern privacy standards. All third-party services and processing are securely proxied through our servers, keeping user details, locations, and travel histories safe and confidential.
